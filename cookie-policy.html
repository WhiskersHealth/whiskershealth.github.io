Cookie Policy – Whiskers Health Organisation

Last updated: September 6, 2025

Whiskers Health Organisation uses cookies and similar technologies on our websites:
• whiskershealth.org
• raffle.whiskershealth.org

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help sites work properly, improve your experience, and provide us with information about site usage.

2. Types of cookies we use

  • Strictly necessary cookies – required for site security, payment processing, and basic functions (e.g., Stripe, PayPal).
  • Functional cookies – remember preferences (e.g., newsletter sign-up state).
  • Analytics cookies – help us understand how visitors use our sites (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Third-party cookies – set by our partners (PayPal, Stripe, Brevo, Supabase, Vercel).

3. Consent

We will ask for your consent before setting any non-essential cookies (such as analytics or marketing cookies). You can manage your preferences via our cookie banner or your browser settings.

4. Managing cookies

You can disable cookies by adjusting your browser settings. Note: blocking essential cookies may affect website functionality (e.g., donations or raffle entries may not process correctly).

5. Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Please check regularly for changes.
