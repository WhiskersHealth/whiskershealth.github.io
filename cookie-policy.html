Cookie Policy

Last updated: September 02, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding cookies on the Whiskers Health Organisation website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience.

How We Use Cookies

  • To remember your preferences and settings.
  • To analyze site traffic and usage (e.g., via analytics tools).
  • To enhance site functionality and performance.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your choices to provide a more personalized experience.

Your Choices

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our site.

More Information

For more details about cookies, visit allaboutcookies.org.
