Privacy Policy – Whiskers Health Organisation

Last updated: September 6, 2025

Whiskers Health Organisation (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a registered charity in England & Wales (No. 1212966) with a registered office at:
5 Brayford Square, London, E1 0SG.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with data protection laws, including the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Information we collect

  • Contact details: when you send us a message through our website (name, email, message).
  • Newsletter sign-ups: when you subscribe to updates.
  • Donations: when you donate via PayPal, we receive transaction details from PayPal.
  • Volunteer and adoption applications: when you apply, we collect additional personal details needed for vetting.
  • Raffle participation: when you take part in our fundraising raffle, we collect your name, email, payment details, and entry information.
  • Automatically collected data: via cookies and analytics (e.g., IP address, browser, device type).

2. How we use your information

  • Respond to your enquiries.
  • Process donations and gift aid (if applicable).
  • Manage volunteer and adoption applications.
  • Send newsletters (where you have subscribed).
  • Run fundraising raffles fairly and securely.
  • Improve our website and services.
  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g., charity reporting).

3. Legal basis for processing

  • Consent: for newsletters and cookies.
  • Contract: to process raffle entries or donations.
  • Legal obligation: for charity compliance and financial reporting.
  • Legitimate interest: to run our organisation and communicate with supporters.

4. Sharing your information

We only share data with trusted service providers that support our work:

  • PayPal – donation payments.
  • Brevo – email newsletters.
  • Web3Forms – contact form submissions.
  • Stripe – raffle payments.
  • Supabase – raffle data storage.
  • Resend – raffle email notifications.
  • Vercel – raffle platform hosting.
  • Google Analytics (if enabled) – website analytics.

We do not sell your data.

5. International transfers

Some providers may store data outside the UK/EEA (e.g., in the USA). Where this happens, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as standard contractual clauses.

6. Your rights

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Request a copy of your data (portability).
  • Withdraw consent (e.g., unsubscribe from newsletters).
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO): https://ico.org.uk/

7. Data retention

  • Contact messages: up to 12 months.
  • Donations/raffle transactions: 6 years (legal/financial recordkeeping).
  • Volunteer/adoption records: as long as you remain involved, plus 3 years.
  • Newsletter subscriptions: until you unsubscribe.

8. Contact us

If you have any questions or wish to exercise your rights, contact us at:
